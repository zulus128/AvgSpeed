AvgSpeed Watch Support

AvgSpeed Watch is a watch-only app that tracks your average speed for running, walking, and cycling, with live metrics and a complication.

  • Compatible with Apple Watch (watchOS 10+).
  • Uses location to compute speed and distance.
  • Uses a workout session for reliable background tracking; workouts are not saved to Health.

Privacy Policy

AvgSpeed Watch does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

  • Location data is used on-device to calculate speed and distance while tracking.
  • No location history is stored or transmitted.
  • HealthKit is used only to start a workout session for background tracking; no health data is read or saved.
  • App settings and the latest average speed are stored locally on your watch.
